The ABM Youth Entrepreneur Club

The ABM Youth Entrepreneur Club is a newly founded club composed of teachers and students of ABM. The Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) created a values with the four principles namely: Integrity, Objectivity, Confidentiality, and Competency, to avoid any unethical actions and lessen the impact of doing such things that violate the school’s rules and regulations. Due to the deviation of ABM students mostly in bringing unnecessary things that give a bad image to the students and the instructors. The aim of this club are to develop new skills, interests in business, to develop their social communication and provide support and encouragement to the students of Philippine College of Advanced Arts and Technology.

Campus Ministry Club

PCAAT Campus Ministry is an organization established to ensure students' moral and spiritual growth. It highlights the engagement of both learners and faculty members in the process of incorporating spiritual guidance and support to one another. Through this organization we aim to build a community of academically excellent and morally upright individuals.

PAPREFU Club

The Past, Present, Future: Social Sciences Club (PAPREFU) is a newly founded club composed of teachers from the department of Social Sciences and Research. Its aim is to encourage the students of PCAAT to enrich their knowledge and fuel their passion for culture and history.

PPAG Club

The Phoenix Performing Arts Guild (PPAG) is a club dedicated to giving students more opportunities to learn and grow in the performing arts. Membership and effective participation will assist students in nurturing and developing 21st-century skills, including social and leadership skills.

Research Club

The PCAAT Guild of Student Researchers is the academic arm of the Department of Research and Social Sciences training students in the pluricentric approach in conducting, writing, and reporting research findings. 

SDRCO Club

The Student Disaster and Rescue, Cadet Officer, serves as an official
organization/club for students who aspire to become SDRC Officers that ensure academic
excellence, uphold discipline and respect to everyone and act as a role model for the entire
students within the institution with pride and dignity.

SSG Damara Club

The SSG is founded on the principles of participatory democracy, responsible servant-leadership, collaboration, unity, accountability, and efficiency in serving the student body.
